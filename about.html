What is the issue with B&BW current candles?

From 2020 to present time, Bath and Body Works has faced several allegations concerning products, specifically candles are causing health problems such as cancer, infertility, among other issues.

The root of these issues come from the current use of paraffin wax which contain specific chemicals that contribute to health problems reported by users

Meet benzene, one of currently used chemicals in B&BW candles. Benzene has been confirmed by the Department of Health and Human Services to be harmful to you and "has determined that benzene causes cancer in humans. Long-term exposure to high levels of benzene in the air can cause leukemia, cancer of the blood-forming organs."

Meet toluene, another one of currently used chemicals in B&BW candles. Touene has been confirmed by the Centers for Diseas Control and Prevention to also be harmful for you and "is irritating to the skin, eyes, and respiratory tract. It can cause systemic toxicity by ingestion or inhalation and is slowly absorbed through the skin."

But why is this important?

Aside from the health issues that stem from the current B&BW, we are aiming to support the Flavor and Fragerance Right to Know Act of 2020. We understand that one of B&BW's area of growth consists of being more transparent, which is where Pure Scents come into play. Here at Pure Scents we promote transparency about B&BW products which can help build trust within the community and overall promote a healthier lifestyle.
